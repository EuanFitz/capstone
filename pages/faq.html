Frequently Asked Questions

What is ClickSafe and how does it work?

ClickSafe is a B2B cybersecurity training platform that uses simulated deepfake phishing attacks — including fake calls, voice messages, and video messages — to train your employees to recognize and respond to modern social engineering threats. We send realistic simulations to your team, then provide feedback and training based on how they respond.

Who is ClickSafe designed for?

ClickSafe is built for businesses of all sizes that want to proactively protect their workforce against AI-powered social engineering attacks. Our platform is especially valuable for organizations in finance, legal, healthcare, and other sectors where sensitive information is frequently targeted.

Are the deepfake simulations safe to use?

Absolutely. All simulations are conducted in a controlled environment and are clearly logged on the backend. No real data is ever compromised, and simulations are designed purely to educate — not to cause harm or distress to employees.

Will employees know they are being tested?

Employees are informed during onboarding that simulated attacks may occur at any time as part of their organization's security training program. This awareness is intentional — it keeps teams vigilant without creating a culture of distrust.

How realistic are the deepfake simulations?

Our simulations use state-of-the-art AI to closely replicate the look, voice, and mannerisms of familiar figures such as executives or colleagues. They are designed to reflect the quality of threats your employees are likely to encounter in the real world.

What types of simulations does ClickSafe offer?

ClickSafe currently offers three simulation formats: deepfaked phone calls, voice messages, and video messages. Each format targets a different attack vector and can be customized to reflect scenarios relevant to your organization.

Can simulations be customized for our organization?

Yes. ClickSafe allows you to tailor simulations based on your company's structure, common communication patterns, and specific threat profiles. Custom scenarios can include spoofed executives, vendors, or IT personnel familiar to your team.

How does ClickSafe measure training effectiveness?

The platform tracks key metrics including response rates, escalation behavior, and reporting accuracy across simulations. Administrators receive detailed dashboards showing individual and team-level performance over time, making it easy to identify gaps and measure improvement.

Is ClickSafe compliant with privacy and data protection laws?

ClickSafe is designed with compliance in mind and adheres to applicable data protection regulations including GDPR and CCPA. We do not store personally identifiable information beyond what is necessary to deliver and measure training outcomes.

How do we get started with ClickSafe?

Getting started is straightforward. After an initial consultation to understand your organization's needs, our team will configure your account, set up your first simulation campaign, and provide onboarding support for your administrators. Most organizations are up and running within a few business days.
